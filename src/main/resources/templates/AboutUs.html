About Oxygen

Oxygen is headquartered in Mumbai, India, and operates a network of branches across the India. Our first facility was established in Kharghar,Navi Mumbai with 3 production units and we have since grown to 12 states. Today our group became a family of 300 employees through a combination of greenfield projects and acquisitions. We believe that the "Oxyzone" brand is strongly associated with our mission to deliver high-quality and affordable products to the broader population by leveraging our economies of scale, skilled engineers, and an efficient business model.



Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high quality affordable products to the broader population in India. Our core values encompasses innovation, efficiency,respect for all and excellence as a culture. At the same time, we seek to generate a strong financial performance and deliver long-term value to our shareholders through the execution of our business strategy.
